## 📝 Description

**The Nest** is a Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to enhance the experience of attendees at the **Her Career Conference**.

Built by the **Intelligent Nest cohort**, the app helps users navigate the event, discover speakers, and explore the community.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Provide a clear and accessible conference agenda
* Enable easy navigation during the event
* Showcase speakers and sessions
* Highlight the cohort and their work
* Integrate an AI assistant for guidance and interaction

---

## 👥 Target Users

* Conference attendees (≈80 participants)
* Women exploring career transitions
* Visitors discovering the cohort after the event

---

## 💡 Product Vision

> Built by women, for women.
> A digital space to navigate, connect, and shape the future of work.

---

## ⚙️ Core Features

### 🗓 Interactive Agenda

* Session schedule with time, room, and speaker
* “What’s happening now” view
* Bookmark sessions (stored locally)

### 🎤 Speaker Profiles

* Name, photo, bio, and role
* Linked sessions

### 🧑‍💻 Cohort Showcase

* Profiles of each team member
* Skills, contributions, and links
* Functions as a live portfolio

### 🤖 Nesta — AI Assistant

* Helps users navigate the conference
* Answers questions
* Captures insights about career paths
